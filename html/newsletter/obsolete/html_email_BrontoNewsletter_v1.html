Bronto Newsletter 1
Well here it is, Bronto’s first-ever newsletter.
At long last we took the time to write our first newsletter, just in time for the new year. Here’s wishing you all a very Bronto holiday season and a Bronto 2011. Enjoy the latest and greatest Bronto news below! For those who wish to unsubscribe click here – we won’t hold it against you.

Forxx
Straight-bladed Chrome-moly Forks
First up, the fantastic UDO fork has been built and tested. Not only does it look cool, it rides cool as well. 26er forks are ready to build, with 29er forks coming not long afterwards. We went with a segmented fork design, it looks burly and it is. These will come stock in matte black, or to perfectly match your frame when ordered together. Price is $299…soon to be available in our web store! Or just send us a mail. Visit
