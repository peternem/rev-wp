Welcome to Reven

Reven is a new bike company based out of Belgium. We are all about racing and suffering in the saddle, and we offer aggressive yet affordable carbon race bikes. Go fast, look good, ride your bike.

We have two road models. The Fury is our premium race bike, a monocoque carbon frame with excellent stiffness to weight, ready for Di2 or cable, tapered fork and head tube, and a gorgeous red and silver race design. This bike will make you go faster.

The Rivet is our performance race bike, slightly more relaxed geometry for longer rides and more value.

We also have two mountain bikes, both carbon hard tails: The Jack and Hammer, one for 29 inch wheels and the other for 27.5.

Check us out at www.revenbikes.com, and follow us on FaceBook.

Welcome Pascal!

Pascal Pauwels is our new man in Belgium, Netherlands, and Luxembourg. He will be on the road selling and promoting Reven Race Bikes in your local shop soon. Pascal has a strong history of racing, repairing, and selling bikes, and is a valuable addition to our team.

Feel free to contact Pascal at info@bicycle-assistance.be.

Team Tacx Racing Germany

Reven is proud to be the official bike of the Tacx Factory racing team in Germany. Eight riders strong, they will be contesting the local elite racing scene in Germany and hunting for wins. Both Fury and Rivet frames have custom Tacx Team designs, and team bikes will also be used at Tacx events across the country!
